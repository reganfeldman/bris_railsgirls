Partners

Rails Girls Brisbane is co-organized with our awesome partners.

Want to help? We're looking for partners & sponsors for the non-profit event! Email us!

Netengine are passionate technologists creating world-class user experiences. They use Ruby on Rails to build awesome web applications. They use the latest open source technologies to solve your business problems. Their applications are enjoyable to use and are available anywhere, on any device. They keep it simple.

Ennova believe great software is borne of great ideas and great execution. We want to hear about your idea, get excited and then build it. We use the best practices Agile has to offer including: test-driven development, continuous integration, rapid releases and close customer collaboration to deliver great software.

River City Labs want to foster innovation and entrepreneurial spirit and venture by providing an affordable co-working environment where our tenants are exposed to all types of events and opportunities they might not find elsewhere.

YOW! is a new conference for applied functional programmers. The Australian functional programming community is getting together for two days in Brisbane May 16-17, 2013 to share experiences, practices through intermediate and advanced talks, jams and workshops.

BrisRuby is a Brisbane Ruby on Rails group for Ruby on Rails users of all experience levels to discuss, demonstrate, and share information, projects, and best practices regarding the Rails framework and the Ruby programming language.

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Please bring your laptop (we'll need to know in your application form whether you're bringing a mac or pc).

Can men attend? Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, girls are given a priority.

I know how to program - How can I help? We’re also looking for people to be coaches. We’ll have a two-three hour workshop before the event to walk you through the curriculum. Email us
